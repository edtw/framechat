## Revolut Referral Program

### How It Works
- **One-sided reward**: Only the referrer (existing customer) earns. The invitee gets nothing.
- **Campaign-based**: Invites appear as in-app tiles; not everyone gets them at all times.
- **Time-boxed**: ~21-day campaign windows with hard deadlines.
- **Maximum referrals**: Typically 5 per campaign period.

### Required Steps for Reward
1. Friend opens a **new Revolut Personal account** via unique link
2. **Top up** account from external source (bank transfer or external card)
3. **Order a physical card** (invitee pays delivery ~5 GBP/EUR)
4. **3 qualifying purchases** of ≥5 GBP/EUR each (excludes gambling, gift cards, FX, P2P)

### Reward Amounts (Variable Rewards Model)
| Probability | Reward (GBP) | Approx EUR |
|-------------|-------------|------------|
| 60% | £10 | ~€12 |
| 26% | £25 | ~€29 |
| 10% | £50 | ~€58-60 |
| 3% | £100 | ~€116 |
| 1% | £200 | ~€232 |

Common promotional campaigns: £40-70 per referral.

### Clawback Conditions
Reward reversed if invitee: closes account within 14 days, cancels card, or refunds a qualifying purchase.

## Revolut in Brazil

### Regulatory Status
- Operates under **SCD (Sociedade de Crédito Direto)** license from Banco Central do Brasil (since mid-2023)
- Full banking license considered "optional" per CEO Glauber Mota
- Brazil was a **test market** for accelerated global rollout

### Services Available
- Checking account in BRL
- Debit card
- Bill payments
- **PIX** (standard + PIX Automático for recurring bills)
- Planned: local credit card, personal loans, investments, subscription benefits

### PIX Specifics
- Local BRL transfers can ONLY be done via PIX (no TED/DOC)
- **PIX Automático**: Free for recurring bills. Authorize companies (CNPJ) to charge on schedule. Set value limits per authorization.
- Receiving PIX into Revolut is **free** (no Revolut fees)

## Common Issues for Brazilian Users

### Top Complaints
1. **Account freezes**: Triggered by unusual activity, large transfers, flagged sources. Resolution: up to 7 business days.
2. **Verification problems**: Rejected documents (expired, handwritten, damaged, mismatched details)
3. **Card cloning/theft**: Multiple reports of contactless card theft in Brazil
4. **ATM failures**: Cards rejected at most Brazilian ATMs except Santander
5. **High fees**: "Taxas altíssimas" complaints on Standard plan
6. **Poor support**: Slow, contradictory responses; 3+ hour chat sessions
7. **Service scaling**: 150% customer growth (2023→2024) but support didn't scale

### How to Escalate for Users
1. In-app chat (24/7) — first step
2. Phone: (11) 5039-1888 (São Paulo) or 0800 591 1445
3. Email: `suporte@revolut.com`
4. Ombudsman: `ouvidoria@revolut.com`
5. Formal complaints: `formalcomplaints@revolut.com`
6. **Banco Central do Brasil** — file a complaint with the Central Bank (often speeds up resolution)
